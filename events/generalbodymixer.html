General Body Mixer

Club Zamana's General Body Mixer is our first event of the year, welcoming the members of the Zamana General Body to meet old and new members alike over delicious Indian food. It's the only event of the year where all the clubs of the South Asian intergroup table together to bring to you what the community has to offer. The mixer is held in Lerner Hall near the beginning of fall semester; whether you're interested in joining a club or just seeing what we're all about, look out for the date!
