The 2015 Tamasha Lineup

Congratulations to the following groups that will be performing at Tamasha!

Raas

Celebrating the energetic style of Garba-Raas, Columbia Raas performs at various competitions around the country.

Dhoom

Columbia's premier Bollywood fusion dance team, Dhoom combines a variety of styles, including classical Indian, hip-hop, ballet, and jazz.

Taal

Taal is Columbia's classical Indian fusion dance team, combining forms of classical Indian dance such as Bharatnatyam and Kathak with western forms.

Bhangra

Performing the popular bhangra dance style, Columbia Bhangra combines traditional and contemporary music and choreography to create entertaining routines.

Sur

Columbia's premier Indian fusion a cappella group, Sur creates sick mash-ups of popular Bollywood and American pop songs.

Raaga

Columbia's newest South Asian musical group, Raaga entertains and inspires audiences with its harmonious and powerful songs.
