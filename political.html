Club Zamana Political Forum 2015

Club Zamana is proud to present its first Political Forum. The event, taking place on February 13, 2015, will feature a mix of talks, discussion forums, and other events on the topic of South Asians in America.

We are excited to announce the following speakers:

Raj Goyle - An Indian Democratic Politician talking about South Asian Relations to Politics in America

Rochelle Almeida - A Masters teacher of Liberal Studies in New York University speaking on The Changing Face of South Asians in America over the past 25 years

Our forum will also feature discussion lead by MSA and Sewa on South Asian Interfaith Issues in America

Lunch will be provided as well. We hope to see you there!
