Hindu Students Organization

The Hindu Students Organization provides a forum for students to practice and explore Hinduism in an atmosphere marked by tolerance, open-mindedness, and a willingness to engage in dialogue. Although Hinduism and its customs take many forms, the HSO focuses on weekly devotional services, pujas on auspicious occasions, seva or service, and a general celebration of Hindu traditions and festivals.
