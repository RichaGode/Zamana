Join Zamana

Throughout the year, Zamana holds a wide range of events for our members and the community, from political forums, movie nights, and community service days to semi formals, career panels, and educational activities. In the spring, Zamana puts on Columbia's largest cultural show, Tamasha, featuring performances from all South Asian dance groups at Columbia, other student performances, and outside acts.

Interested in joining Club Zamana? We're looking for new Columbia and Barnard students to join the board as an organizational committee member (OCM). As an OCM, you'll be an integral part of Zamana's board by planning events, assisting in Zamana finances, and serving as a voice for the South Asian community on campus. OCMs will be required to attend weekly board meetings on Wednesdays at 10 PM.

If you're interested in attending a casual, informational interview with the board, click on the button below. We're excited to meet you!
